Our Vision

Our school seeks to prepare a distinguished student scientifically, morally, and educationally, keeping pace with his time, belonging to his creative country, in light of a healthy, educational and safe environment and effective community participation.

Our mission

Learning through fun and discovery because this creates in the child the ability to innovate and express himself.

Our Goal

I wish for continuous training for teachers. In fact, we are really working on this, and for training for teachers and students to become the language of life. Also for everyone to have a continuous reading, for reading is life.
Hanaa Roshdy - School Principal

The mission of Al-Salam Private Language School is to provide each student with a diverse education in a safe, supportive enviroment that promotes self-discipline, motivation, and excellence in learning. Our Team joins the parents and community to assist students in developing skills to become independent and self-sufficient adults who will succeed and contribute responsibility in a global community.
